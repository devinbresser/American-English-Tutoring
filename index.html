Welcome to American English Tutoring with Linda

Hi, I'm Linda! My passion is teaching English! An American, I have lived in many countries teaching ESL. Now in the USA, I am a certified teacher with a Master's Degree in Teaching English. I'm also TESOL certified. My goal is to help you become a fluent American English speaker! I also love yoga, walking with my dogs, reading, and gardening.

Services and rates available

I offer all levels of American English lessons from beginner to advanced conversation classes. Lessons are custom designed to meet you where you are and to help you achieve your English language goals. All areas of language learning are addressed: listening, speaking, reading, and writing. New vocabulary is introduced in each lesson.
